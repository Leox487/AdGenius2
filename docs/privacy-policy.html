AdGenius Privacy Policy

Marketing Content Discovery Tool

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

AdGenius collects minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Search Queries: Product names and industries you search for
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service
  • Authentication Data: Basic login information for service access

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized ad discovery results
  • Improve our service functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensure service security and prevent abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure authentication methods

5. Third-Party Services

Our service may integrate with third-party platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, etc.) to provide content discovery. These platforms have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to provide our service functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that could identify individual users.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.
